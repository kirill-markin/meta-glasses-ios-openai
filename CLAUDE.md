# AI Glasses

iOS app for experimenting with Meta Ray-Ban smart glasses.

## Stack

- Swift / SwiftUI
- SwiftData (local persistence)
- CloudKit (sync)
- Meta Glasses SDK (Facebook)

## Purpose

Experimental project to explore Meta Ray-Ban glasses capabilities:
- Camera: photo and video capture
- Audio: recording and playback
- Streaming via Meta SDK
- Integration with iOS companion app
